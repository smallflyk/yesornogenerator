1. What Is Yes or No Wheel?

The Yes or No Wheel is a random yes or no generator. This decision wheel focuses specifically on yes or no answers. It's a specialized spinner derived from Picker Wheel, sometimes called the Yes No Picker Wheel.

By clicking the "SPIN" button on the Yes or No Wheel, you will get either yes or no at the end of the spin. This helps you make decisions quickly using the Yes or No Wheel.

There are 2 input modes available for the Yes or No Wheel: standard "yes no" and expanded "yes no maybe" inputs.

2. How to Use the Yes or No Wheel?

You can use this Yes or No Wheel generator whenever you want to make a quick decision. Simply spin the Yes or No Wheel and let it decide for you, eliminating the need to rely on others for your choices.

Below are the steps to use the Yes or No Wheel effectively:

  1. Choose a Yes or No Wheel mode:
    • Yes or No only
    • Yes, No, or Maybe
  2. Choose the number of input sets for the Yes or No Wheel:
    • From 1 set to 5 sets
  3. Click the "SPIN" button or press "Ctrl + Enter" keys to spin the Yes or No Wheel and generate your result.
  4. Your Yes or No Wheel result will display and be added to your tally.
  5. Continue spinning the Yes or No Wheel if needed for further confirmation.
  6. Click the hide button to minimize the Inputs section and center the Yes or No Wheel.
  7. Use the reset button to clear your Yes or No Wheel results history.

The Yes or No Wheel is incredibly easy and quick to use. You can customize your Yes or No Wheel, view your spin history, and even enter full-screen mode for a better experience.

11. Need an Alternative to Yes or No Wheel?
